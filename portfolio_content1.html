Self-portrait

A simple portrait using multiple light sources. The intention was to utilize colour and post-processing to create a striking photo that stands out.
Playing with levels, exclusion layers, skewing and other effects in Photoshop, I gave the image a VCR-like effect. The image was also first stripped of all colour, then using split-toning, I added a pink colour to the highlights, and a green/blue colour to the shadows.
